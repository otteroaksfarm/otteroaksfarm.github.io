Spring Planting Guide

Welcome to our spring planting season! This year we're focusing on expanding our native flower varieties and implementing new sustainable growing techniques. The mild winter has given us a head start, and we're excited to share our progress with you.

Our planting schedule this year includes:

  • Early spring: Peonies, ranunculus, and anemones
  • Mid-spring: Zinnias, cosmos, and sunflowers
  • Late spring: Dahlias and specialty cut flowers
April 28, 2024

Flower Arranging Workshop Recap

Our April flower arranging workshop was our most successful yet, with 25 participants learning to create beautiful spring bouquets. We used a mix of early blooms from our fields, including tulips, daffodils, and flowering branches.

Highlights from the workshop:

  • Demonstration of spiral bouquet technique
  • Tips for extending vase life of cut flowers
  • Creating texture with different foliage types
