March 10, 2024

Our Sustainable Farming Practices

At Otter Oaks Farm, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do.

This year we've implemented several new practices:

  • Companion planting to reduce pests naturally
  • Rainwater collection system installation
  • Transition to 100% organic fertilizers

We believe these changes will help us reduce our environmental impact while producing healthier flowers and plants.
