Our Story

Welcome to Otter Oaks Farm, a small family farm in Maple Park, Illinois. We specialize in sustainably grown cut flowers, herbs, and small batch farm products.

Our farm was established in 2018 with the mission of providing our community with beautiful, chemical-free flowers and farm goods while practicing regenerative agriculture.

What began as a small backyard garden has blossomed into a thriving farm that serves our local community through:

  • Seasonal flower subscriptions
  • Farm-fresh products
  • Educational workshops
  • Community events
  • Volunteer opportunities

We believe in farming practices that nurture the land, support pollinators, and create a sustainable future for our community.

Volunteer With Us

We welcome volunteers to join us on the farm! Whether you're looking to learn about sustainable farming, get some fresh air, or just be part of our community, we'd love to have you.

Volunteer opportunities include:

  • Planting and harvesting flowers
  • Weeding and garden maintenance
  • Farm stand assistance
  • Event setup and support
  • Special projects
